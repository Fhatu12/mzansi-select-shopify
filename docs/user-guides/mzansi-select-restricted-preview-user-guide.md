# Mzansi Select restricted preview user guide

**Document Type:** Internal user guide  
**Prepared:** 2026-05-19  
**Owner:** Product Owner  
**Status:** Restricted preview guide for internal use only. This is not a public launch guide. It preserves the current restricted-preview posture and must not be read as evidence that products are available for purchase. Some browse/link refinements accepted locally in **Slice 21BQ** still require preview-side rendered confirmation; where relevant, this guide labels them as the **current preview expectation** or **to be confirmed**.  
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Document status

- This is a **restricted preview guide**.
- This guide is for **internal use only**.
- This is **not** a public launch guide.
- This guide is for internal reviewers and stakeholders, not public customers.

## 2. What Mzansi Select is in this preview

In the current restricted preview, Mzansi Select is presented as a curated South African-facing Shopify storefront preview.

The preview is intended for safe internal review only. It is a browse-and-feedback surface, not a live trading storefront. Products, pricing, delivery wording, and supporting service surfaces must be treated as preview material unless separately confirmed.

## 3. How to access the preview

- Use authorised preview access only.
- Use only the approved restricted-preview access path shared internally.
- Do not include or store passwords, tokens, cookies, secrets, or raw auth/session files in notes, screenshots, commits, or documents.
- Do not share preview access publicly.
- Do not forward preview links outside the approved internal reviewer group.

## 4. What reviewers can do

Reviewers may use the restricted preview to:

- browse homepage sections
- use approved navigation and department links
- inspect product cards and product pages where available
- review both mobile and desktop experience
- compare wording, clarity, and trust signals across the current preview
- provide feedback using the existing reviewer pack, feedback template, and review-session material

## 5. What reviewers must not do

- Do not attempt checkout or payment.
- Do not treat products as orderable.
- Do not change Shopify Admin.
- Do not install apps.
- Do not import or sync products.
- Do not share preview links publicly.
- Do not enter real customer, order, or payment data.
- Do not upload, copy, or store supplier credentials, passwords, tokens, cookies, or raw session material.

## 6. Navigation guide

### 6.1 Header search posture

- The header search is a reviewable browse surface.
- Search currently runs across preview items.
- The desktop category filter is intentionally paused rather than presented as a live department filter.
- If search behaviour or search-result wording feels misleading, record it as feedback.

### 6.2 Approved department quartet

The current approved department quartet is:

- Home & Living
- Kitchen & Storage
- Office & Desk
- Tech Accessories

These are the primary approved browse departments in the current preview.

### 6.3 Deferred departments

These departments remain deferred in the current preview:

- Garden & Outdoor
- Bath & Bedroom
- Cleaning & Laundry

They should be treated as coming-later or deferred states, not live browse areas.

### 6.4 Bottom bar and mobile drawer behaviour

- On mobile, reviewers should inspect the drawer and bottom bar as part of the intended browse shell.
- Home and search remain the key active mobile browse controls.
- Wishlist, account, and cart surfaces remain deferred or paused.
- Browse-link specificity introduced in the latest local route-honesty work is the **current preview expectation** and should be confirmed in rendered preview where possible.

## 7. Product browsing guide

- Product rails are browse and review surfaces.
- Product cards are not purchase controls.
- Live product-backed cards may open product pages where available.
- Static preview cards may remain non-purchasable review surfaces.
- `Add to Cart` and `Buy Now` remain unavailable.
- Some prices, delivery details, and supplier details may remain preview-only, deferred, or to be confirmed.
- Placeholder pricing or wording such as price-to-confirm should be treated as intentional preview honesty, not launch-ready commerce data.

## 8. Preview-only states to expect

Reviewers should expect preview-only or deferred states such as:

- deferred links
- `Coming later` labels
- disabled newsletter capture
- account deferred states
- wishlist deferred states
- cart deferred states
- support/helper surfaces that remain paused until a later approved stage

If any of these surfaces look active, final, or customer-ready when they are not, capture that as feedback.

## 9. Mobile review guidance

On mobile, reviewers should check:

- the mobile header
- the drawer
- the bottom bar
- product-card readability
- section spacing
- whether deferred states remain understandable rather than confusing

Also note whether the preview still feels browse-first and non-purchasable on a small screen.

## 10. Desktop review guidance

On desktop, reviewers should check:

- the header and navigation
- the hero area
- the category strip
- product rails
- promo, trust, and footer sections
- whether browse links feel specific and honest
- whether paused states still read clearly as paused

## 11. Feedback guidance

Feedback is most useful when it focuses on:

- clarity
- trust
- wording
- mobile readability
- product fit
- confusing or misleading states

Reviewers should also note:

- anything that feels too final
- anything that implies products can be bought now
- anything that weakens trust
- any mismatch between expected and actual route behaviour

Use the existing internal review material:

- `docs/pilot/slice-21bb-restricted-preview-reviewer-pack.md`
- `docs/pilot/slice-21bc-restricted-preview-feedback-capture.md`
- `docs/pilot/slice-21bd-restricted-preview-review-session-plan.md`

## 12. Known limitations

- This is a restricted preview.
- Checkout, payment, and customer-order flow are not part of this preview.
- Newsletter capture is disabled.
- Account, wishlist, and cart remain deferred or paused.
- This is not a public launch.
- Some product prices, delivery wording, supplier signals, and supporting details remain non-final.
- Some route-honesty refinements accepted locally in **Slice 21BQ** still require preview-side rendered validation and should be treated cautiously until confirmed.
- Where there is uncertainty, the correct reading is **current preview expectation** or **to be confirmed**, not confirmed live behaviour.

## 13. Sensitivity and privacy

Exclude all sensitive material from feedback, screenshots, notes, exports, and commits.

Do not include:

- passwords
- secrets
- tokens
- cookies
- customer data
- order data
- payment data
- supplier credentials
- raw auth/session files

Also do not use real customer, order, or payment information anywhere in the preview.

## 14. Suggested reviewer flow

1. Access the preview through the approved internal route only.
2. Confirm that the session remains a restricted preview and not a live storefront.
3. Review the homepage on desktop and mobile.
4. Open the approved department links and inspect whether the browse behaviour matches the current preview expectation.
5. Review product cards and available product pages without treating them as purchasable.
6. Check paused states such as newsletter, account, wishlist, cart, and deferred support links.
7. Record feedback with the existing review pack and template.

## 15. Review posture reminders

- Treat all browse surfaces as review surfaces first.
- Treat product cards as content and routing checks, not order controls.
- Treat delivery, pricing, and supplier wording cautiously unless specifically confirmed.
- Escalate anything that makes the preview look publicly launched, purchasable, or more final than the accepted source truth allows.
